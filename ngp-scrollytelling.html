Reconstitution Guide

Mixing with Precision.

Every self-mix vial is supplied with 2 ml bacteriostatic water. The reconstitution process is simple — and critical for preserving peptide integrity.

The golden rule: never shake. Shaking creates shear forces that break peptide chains. Always swirl gently. Inject bacteriostatic water down the vial wall, not directly onto the powder.

1
Draw 2 ml of bacteriostatic water into a syringe
2
Inject slowly down the inside wall of the vial — do not spray directly onto powder
3
Swirl gently until fully dissolved — never shake or vortex
4
Allow to sit 2–3 minutes if powder does not dissolve immediately
5
Refrigerate immediately after reconstitution — use within the stated window
